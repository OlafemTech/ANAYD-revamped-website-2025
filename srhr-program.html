Sexual and Reproductive Health Rights (SRHR) Program

ANAYD is dedicated to improving access to comprehensive sexual and reproductive health services for adolescents and young people across Africa, ensuring they have the knowledge and resources to make informed decisions about their health.

Key SRHR Projects and Initiatives

Social Networking Testing (SNT) Project

A USD51,000 EGPAF-supported initiative focused on HIV Testing Services for adolescents and young people:

  • Coverage: 9 Local Government Areas across Benue, Lagos, and Rivers states
  • Target: Reaching 71,000 adolescents and young people with HIV Testing Services
  • Duration: 6 months (April-September 2023)
  • Partner: Elizabeth Glaser Pediatric AIDS Foundation (EGPAF)

HIV Self Testing Initiative

A comprehensive HIV self-testing program implemented in Lagos State:

  • Achievement Highlights:
    • 18,500 people reached with HIVST information
    • 18,500 HIV self-testing kits distributed
    • Comprehensive result tracking and follow-up support
  • Coverage: Lagos State
  • Partner: JHPIEGO
  • Value: USD 57,205

Our Approach to SRHR

Comprehensive Education

We provide comprehensive sexuality education and information about sexual and reproductive health rights to empower informed decision-making.

Healthcare Access

We facilitate access to quality sexual and reproductive health services, including HIV testing and counseling services.

Peer Support

We build networks of peer educators and supporters to provide youth-friendly information and guidance on SRHR issues.

Rights Advocacy

We advocate for policies and programs that protect and promote the sexual and reproductive health rights of young people.
