Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Improving our services and user experience

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to work properly.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to improve our services.

Functional Cookies

Enhance website functionality and personalization based on your preferences and interactions.

Marketing Cookies

Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness.
