Malaria Program

ANAYD's malaria program focuses on prevention, early detection, and treatment support across Nigeria and West Africa. Through community education and resource distribution, we work to reduce malaria incidence among young people.

Prevention Education

Comprehensive malaria prevention education through community outreach, focusing on environmental management and personal protection measures.

Treatment Support

Access to rapid diagnostic tests and antimalarial medications through our network of healthcare partners and mobile clinics.

Tuberculosis Program

ANAYD's tuberculosis program focuses on early detection, prevention, and treatment support. We work to reduce TB transmission and improve treatment outcomes through comprehensive community-based interventions.

Early Detection

Comprehensive TB screening and diagnostic services through mobile clinics and partnership with healthcare facilities.

Treatment Support

Treatment adherence support and monitoring through community health workers and support groups.

Gender-Based Violence Program

ANAYD's Gender-Based Violence program provides comprehensive support services, prevention education, and advocacy for survivors of gender-based violence while working to address root causes and promote gender equality.

Prevention & Education

Community-based education and awareness programs focusing on GBV prevention, gender equality, and healthy relationships.

Support Services

Comprehensive support for survivors including counseling, safe spaces, legal aid, and access to medical services.
