Our Approach

Early Detection

We provide access to modern TB diagnostic services and screening programs, ensuring early detection and timely treatment initiation.

Treatment Support

Our treatment support program helps patients complete their TB medication course through counseling and monitoring.

Community Education

We conduct community awareness programs to educate about TB prevention, symptoms, and the importance of seeking medical care.

Stigma Reduction

We work to reduce TB-related stigma through education and community engagement, ensuring patients feel supported throughout their treatment journey.
