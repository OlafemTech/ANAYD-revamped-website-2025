Digital Skills Training Program

ANAYD is committed to empowering young people with essential digital skills through comprehensive training programs and skill acquisition initiatives.

Key Digital Skills Initiatives

Socio-Economic Tailoring Skill Empowerment Project

In collaboration with EHRAAI, we implemented a comprehensive skill empowerment project:

  • Duration: 4 months (September - December 2020)
  • Target Group: Key and vulnerable groups living with HIV impacted by COVID-19
  • Impact: 10 Adolescents and Young People living with HIV received free scholarship
  • Support: Startup loan provision for business establishment

Young Emerging Leaders Project

A Global Network of Young People Living with HIV (Y+) supported initiative:

  • Budget: USD 6,400
  • Duration: 6 months (June-December 2023)
  • Focus: Leadership development and capacity building
  • Scope: National level implementation

Our Approach to Digital Skills Training

Technical Training

We provide hands-on training in essential digital skills and modern technologies to enhance employability.

Entrepreneurship Support

We offer business development training and startup support to help young people establish their own ventures.

Mentorship

We connect trainees with experienced professionals for guidance and career development support.

Resource Access

We provide access to essential tools, equipment, and financial resources needed for skill development.
