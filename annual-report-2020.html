EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The 2020 Annual Report marks the inaugural year of the African Network of Adolescents and Young Persons Development (ANAYD). Established with a vision to empower adolescents and young people across Africa, ANAYD has laid a strong foundation for addressing key issues affecting youth, including HIV/AIDS awareness, sexual and reproductive health education, and digital advocacy.

Despite the challenges posed by the global COVID-19 pandemic, ANAYD successfully established its organizational structure, formed initial partnerships, and launched several pilot programs. Our focus on creating a youth-led, youth-centered approach has guided our work from inception, ensuring that young people remain at the heart of our mission and activities.

This report outlines our achievements, challenges, and lessons learned during our founding year. It also presents our strategic priorities for the future as we work towards creating a more inclusive, healthy, and empowered generation of young Africans.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS OF 2020 INCLUDE:

  • • Establishing ANAYD as a registered non-profit organization with a clear mission and vision
  • • Building a core team of dedicated young professionals and volunteers
  • • Launching pilot programs in HIV/AIDS awareness and sexual health education
  • • Forming initial partnerships with local community organizations and international agencies
  • • Developing a strategic plan for 2021-2023 to guide our growth and impact

Our Foundation

Organizational Structure

In our founding year, we established the core organizational structure that would support our future growth:

  • Formed a diverse board of directors with expertise in youth development, healthcare, and education
  • Recruited a core team of 12 dedicated professionals
  • Developed our strategic five-year plan for continental expansion

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower adolescents and young people across Africa through health education, advocacy, and skills development, enabling them to lead healthy, productive lives and contribute to their communities.

Our Vision

A continent where all adolescents and young people have equal access to health services, education, and opportunities to reach their full potential.

Core Values

Youth-Centered

Prioritizing the needs and voices of young people

Equity

Ensuring fair access to resources and opportunities

Collaboration

Working together with diverse stakeholders

Inclusivity

Embracing diversity in all its forms

Looking Forward

As we conclude our founding year, we look ahead to 2021 with ambitious goals and a clear roadmap for growth. Building on the foundation established in 2020, we are committed to expanding our reach, deepening our impact, and strengthening our organizational capacity.

1 Geographic Expansion

Expand our operations to at least 5 African countries, establishing local partnerships and adapting our programs to meet specific regional needs.

2 Program Development

Develop comprehensive training programs for youth advocates and expand our HIV/AIDS awareness and sexual health education initiatives.

3 Digital Platform

Launch our digital platform for youth engagement, advocacy, and resource sharing, leveraging technology to reach more young people.

4 Sustainable Funding

Diversify our funding sources by securing multi-year grants, building corporate partnerships, and developing a sustainable donor base.

Our 2021 Targets

  • • Reach 10,000+ young people through our programs and digital platforms
  • • Train 100 youth advocates as peer educators and community leaders
  • • Establish partnerships with at least 10 new organizations across Africa
  • • Secure $750,000 in funding to support our expanded programs
